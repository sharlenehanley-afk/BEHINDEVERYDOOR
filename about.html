About

Hi, I'm Sharlene.
I see spaces differently.

My background

For more than twenty years I've worked in brand strategy, helping businesses understand how they're perceived and why people make the decisions they do.

Over time I noticed something.

The same principles that shape powerful brands also shape powerful places.

Some homes attract stronger emotional responses than others.

Some hotels become memorable while others are quickly forgotten.

Some properties seem to offer everything on paper yet fail to connect.

The difference is rarely one thing.

It's the experience people have as they move through a space, and the story they take away from it.

That's what led me to create Behind Every Door.

What I bring

Strategy, property and perception.

Brand Strategy

I understand how perception is built, how people make decisions and how small signals can change the way something is valued.

Property Experience

As a qualified home stager and former estate agent, I understand how spaces are viewed, judged and remembered in the real world.

Independent Perspective

I look at a space from the outside in - how it feels, what it communicates and where it may not be landing as intended.

Why this matters

The property matters. But the person matters more.

A seller may be planning their next chapter.

A buyer may be deciding where they'll build a life.

A host may be trying to create an experience guests remember long after they've gone home.

Behind Every Door exists to help people see the space, the story and the decision more clearly.

It's not interior design. It's not estate agency. It's not traditional hospitality consultancy.

It's about understanding what a place is communicating - and helping it communicate more clearly.

The BED Lens

A way of reading what a space is saying before deciding what should change.

Based in the UK. Working with sellers, buyers, hosts and property owners who want to understand their space more clearly.

Behind every door there's a story, a potential, a person.

My role is to help uncover it.
